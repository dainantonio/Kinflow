# Orbit Privacy Policy (Family Data)

_Last updated: 2026-02-27_

Orbit is a family coordination app for tasks, schedules, meals, rewards, and messaging. This policy explains the data we process and how families can control it.

## Data We Process
- Account/profile information (name, role, avatar, initials).
- Family workspace data (tasks, events, meals, rewards, chat messages, points).
- Device/app diagnostics (basic logs and usage events for reliability).

## Children and Family Data
- Orbit is intended for parent-managed family groups.
- Parents/admins are responsible for creating and managing child profiles.
- Child data is shown only within the family workspace according to role-based permissions.

## How Data Is Used
- To provide core features (planning, reminders, progress tracking, messaging).
- To sync household data across signed-in devices.
- To improve product quality and security.

## Data Sharing
- Orbit does **not** sell personal data.
- Data is only shared with service providers needed to operate the app (e.g., hosting, auth, database) under contractual safeguards.

## Data Retention
- Family data is retained until deleted by account owners/admins.
- Backups/log retention may persist for a limited operational period.

## Security
- We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards.
- No internet service is risk-free; users should use strong passwords and keep devices secure.

## Family Controls
- Parent/admin users can add, edit, and remove family members.
- Parents/admins can edit/delete family records where permissions allow.
- Families can request deletion by removing profiles/data from the app workspace.

## AI Assistance
- Optional AI features are assistive and should be reviewed by parents before use.
- Avoid entering highly sensitive personal data in free-form prompts.

## Contact
For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact the project maintainers.
